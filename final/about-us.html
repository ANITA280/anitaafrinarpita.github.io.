Sustainibility

We package all our products with sustainable packaging, using recycled plastic to package the food baskets and ship them. In addition the company wants to push for electric/hybrid trucks and vans. This helps us enable to reduce our carbon footprint on the planet as well as reduce carbon emissions emitted by our delivery vehciles. Fresh Organic does not condone the use of plastic which has n it been recycled as we want our carbon footprint will be reduced and help the planet go green.

Who We Are

The founders of Fresh Organic wanted to ensure that customers would receive the finest and most fresh produce from our farms to their homes. The idea of this helped kickstart the business and grow to where it is today. The founders of Fresh Organic had one goal, that goal was to make sure that their custoers were able to consume the most fresh produce. At Fresh Organic we love what we do and we're passionate about the benefits of living a healthy lifestyle, sourcing the highest quality organic produce for our customers and providing the very best home delivery service. We wanted to provide the best nutritional start to life with high quality food free from pesticides, chemicals and artificial hormones. We wanted to access fresh organic produce online and have it home delivered, but at that time, the ideal service we were after was hard to find.

What We Do

At Fresh Organic we care about our customers and what they want. We want to promote organic and fresh produce to our consumers thus allowing us to market a healthier lifestyle to our consuers. As well as we want to promote our local farmers and local agriculture in order to ensure that our doemstic market in agriculture will take off. We want to ensure that the local farming industry in Victoria is appreciated and made sure that all the produced is locally sourced and packaged. Allowing a boom and an increment in employment and contribute tot the GDP.
